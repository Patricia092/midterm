DC

For starters it is the comic book franchise that created Batman, which has created many characters in the DC universe. This has led to several spinoff and endless possibilities for crossovers. There have also been several series that have been derived from the DC universe. There are so many dynamic characters such as the Joker and Harley Quinn that were able to have their own series that appeal to a variety of audiences. Even the Titans characters have several TV series dedicated to a variety of audiences such as Teen Titans, Teen Titans Go, and the most recent adaptation, Titans (2020).
